FAQ

What the bot does and does not do.

Designed for research workflow discipline, not trade execution or guaranteed signals.

Does it place trades?

No. It is research-only. It does not unlock trading, make payments, or place orders.

Can free users use AI tokens?

No. Free `/scan`, `/report`, and `/debate` return cached example content.

Does `/debate` include portfolio risk?

Only for premium users who added a portfolio. Users without a portfolio see non-portfolio research or examples.

What can premium users upload?

CSV, image, or text in private chat through `/addportfolio`, within quota.

Is it financial advice?

No. It is decision support and research organization only.
